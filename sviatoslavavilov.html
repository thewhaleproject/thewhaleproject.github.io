Sviatoslav Avilov

Music Producer, Programmer

Sviatoslav Avilov is the concept director of The Whale Project, the composer and producer of the music and devoper of the interactive visuals.


Designs interactive fractal visualisation programs based on his original PhD thesis developments, currently being defended at the Voronezh State University in the Physical faculty (Demo video of Plato software, 3D particle painting program demo).

Other projects:
Music Composition and Sound design for Repeating Eutrophia International Environmental Dance Performance and Screening project
